Justin Bobby Brescia of The Hills: Where Is He Now? Watch Him Cut Hair

Publish date: 2024-04-25

What happens when you let The Hills’ Justin Bobby Brescia cut your hair? Well, considering “truth and time tells all,” Us Weekly editor Rachel McRady decided to let the former reality TV bad boy show off his styling skills on her tresses in a new exclusive video. 

It turns out, Audrina Patridge’s former love, 33, was a hairdresser long before he stepped in front of a camera. Brescia has been cutting hair since age 14 and used to style Adam Levine’s hair. 

“It’s cool. We have similar attitudes, you know, musician/musician kind of thing,” Brescia tells Us of cutting The Voice coach’s dark locks. 

Now that’s he’s had several years away from reality TV, the California native has moved to New York to focus on his passions: music and cutting hair. He formed the two-man band Bobby Rock with his pal Dave Dariani, and the group recently returned from performing at Austin, Texas’ SXSW Festival. 

But that doesn’t mean he’s neglecting his hair clients. After opening stores in Manhattan Beach, Calif. and even in Nicaragua, where he briefly lived, Brescia’s latest “hair chair” is located right in his Manhattan loft, where he also sells his Brush Your Hair hair products.
