Ariana Grandes 7 Rings Is Getting Boycotted And She Loves It Heres Why!

Publish date: 2024-03-04

It turns out that Ariana Grande’s fans have decided to boycott 7 Rings and she is not disappointed! In fact, she supports it! But why are people begging everyone to stop streaming the song?

It is very funny and ironic, actually! The Arianators are trying to get her latest single, Break Up With Your Girlfriend, I’m Bored, to number one but since the spot is currently taken by 7 Rings…you get the point!

Therefore, a big number of fans took to Twitter to use a ‘boycott seven rings’ hashtag that went on to trend on the platform and get Ariana’s attention!

‘Y’all are so insane and funny. i love u. nothing’s funnier than ‘boycott seven rings’….. i can’t lmao. whatever is meant to be will be, babies i ain’t goin nowhere #thankunext,’ Ariana tweeted in response. Aww….

This hilarious attempt to get Grande’s newest song to the number 1 spot comes after her last two singles topped the charts as well and it looks like the fans really want to keep the tradition going and have each of her releases do the same.

‘Shut up. we aiming for that 3rd #1,’ one follower tweeted at Ariana.

‘Break up with your rehearsals, we’re bored,’ another posted.

Funnily enough, someone even pleaded with Ariana to tweet her support for the boycott of her own song and she replied with ‘r u ok.’

‘Can u please tweet #BOYCOTT7RINGS to show your solidarity?’
