Bronnie Has Released &quot;Screw You&quot; Lyric Video

Publish date: 2024-05-29

Ahead of supporting Over Atlantic in London, as well as the Alstar Xmas Party Tour; Bronnie has dropped a lyric video for original song “Screw You”.

This song will definitely appear on her set during her stage performance. It is usually a crowd-pleaser.

Bronnie asks for audience involvement whenever she performs this song live. With the help of the lyric video, her fans will know all the words before she starts to sing the song.

Watch The Lyric Video To Bronnie’s “Screw You” Here:

The song is sung acoustically and is introduced with her usual larger-than-life personality. She revealed that the song will definitely be a part of her set.

At one point, in the song, she plays a “disgusting chord”, but rather than editing it out or starting the video again, she continues. It makes the video much more personal and interactive with her audience; proving to fans, once again, how reals she really is.

Her BronDogs have already started sharing it out on social media, as well as telling Bronnie how much they love this original song.
