Hayden Christensen: Rachel Bilson Was Not a Fan of My Mustache

Publish date: 2024-04-13

So… no Movember? Hayden Christensen joked to HuffPost Live on Wednesday, Sept. 9, that his girlfriend, Rachel Bilson, was not a fan of his mustache, which the actor grew for his role as Don Piper in the film, 90 Minutes In Heaven.

“It was absolutely 100 percent real,” Christensen said of his facial hair. “And I worked very hard on that mustache. Uh, facial hair isn’t really my thing. I don’t grow it very well. That was a good two months of commitment.”

Even then, he needed some help. “Yeah, there was a little bit of makeup… to make it look a little thicker, but the real Don Piper had a pretty hefty mustache,” baby Briar Rose’s dad mused of his character. “It wasn’t necessarily my thing, it itched a lot. I’m very happy to shave it off.”

His longtime girlfriend Bilson was just as relieved, he revealed to HuffPost. “She was very happy for me to get rid of it. But yeah, it’s the character,” he explained.

Christensen portrays Piper in the film adaptation of the pastor’s 2004 New York Times best-selling book. In his memoir, Piper detailed his near-death experience in heaven after a scary car crash, in which he was pronounced dead on the scene after his heart stopped beating.

The actor also opened up to Us Weekly last week about why he chose this particular role.
