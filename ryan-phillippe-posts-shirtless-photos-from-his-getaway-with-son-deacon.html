Ryan Phillippe Posts Shirtless Photos from His Getaway with Son Deacon

Publish date: 2024-02-23

Ryan Phillippe is having a fun getaway with his son Deacon!

The 46-year-old actor and the 17-year-old budding music artist are on a father-son trip in New Mexico and Ryan shared some photos from their day at the McCauley Warm Springs.

“the rocky uphill hike was well worth the warm woodland baptism that followed,” Ryan captioned a series of photos on his Instagram account.

Click inside to check out more…

Deacon commented on the post and said, “And the viddles.”

Ryan has been in New Mexico while filming the upcoming MacGruber series, which will stream on Peacock in the near future.

In the series, “after rotting in prison for over a decade, America’s ultimate hero and uber patriot MacGruber (Will Forte) is finally released. His mission: to take down a mysterious villain from his past—Brigadier Commander Enos Queeth (Mickey Rourke). With the entire world in the crosshairs, MacGruber must reassemble his old team, Vicki St. Elmo (Kristen Wiig) and Dixon Piper (Phillippe) in order to defeat the forces of evil.”

The father-son duo was spotted working out together a few months ago!
