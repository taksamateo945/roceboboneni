Elevation Worship Here as in Heaven Lyrics

Publish date: 2024-06-26

Elevation Worship is an American contemporary Christian worship music band that originated from Elevation Church in Charlotte, North Carolina. The band’s main aim is to create music that inspires people to connect with God and to deepen their faith.

The band was formed in 2006 by Pastor Steven Furtick, who felt a need for original worship songs for his church and began writing and producing them with the help of a group of musicians. Since then, the band has grown into a popular and influential force in the Christian music industry, releasing over 20 albums and leading worship at events and conferences across America and other parts of the world.

Elevation Worship’s music is known for its passionate and energetic sound, blending elements of rock, pop, and electronic music with powerful lyrics that express themes of faith, hope, and redemption. Some of their most popular songs include “Here as in Heaven,” “O Come to the Altar,” “Do It Again,” and “Graves into Gardens.”

The band is made up of several talented musicians, including Chris Brown, Mack Brock, Tiffany Hammer, Jane Williams, and many others. Elevation Worship’s continued success and impact on the Christian music scene has made them one of the most respected and beloved worship bands in the world today.
